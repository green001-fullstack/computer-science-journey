# What traversal means.

### It means the visiting of all the nodes in a tree

# Why trees have multiple traversals.

### Becasue trees have different branches and the order in which every node is visited will determine their outcome. Different outcomes also have different usecase

# The four major traversal types.

### Preorder
### Inorder
### Postorder
### Level order

# One real-world analogy that helps you remember traversals.

### File Explorer

#### Preorder:

##### Open the folder.
##### Then explore everything inside.

### House

#### Postorder:

##### Clean every room first.
##### Then clean the hallway.
